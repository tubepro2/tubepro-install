Installation instructions

Step 1: Install Scriptable

Scriptable is required in order for TubePro 2 to operate.
Download Scriptable on the App Store

Step 2: Install TubePro 2 shortcut

First, you must enable untrusted shortcuts.

After that's done, click the link below and add the shortcut. If prompted, select "Allow Untrusted Shortcut".
Install Shortcut

Step 3: Adding TubePro 2 to your share menu

From a YouTube video, press "Share" and then tap "More" to bring up the share sheet. Scroll to the bottom, select "Edit Actions", and add TubePro 2 to your favorites.

Skipping ads with TubePro 2

While the pre-roll ads are playing, simply share your video to TubePro 2 and the video will immediately start playing without ads!

Playing videos in the background

Share a video to TubePro 2, then lock your device and press the play button.
